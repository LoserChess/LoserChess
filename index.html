Rules:

  • All pieces move the same as normal chess
  • The winner of the game is the first player to lose all of their pieces
  • The game ends in a draw if neither player has a legal move, the same board position is repeated 3 times in 1 game or if in a 50 move span no player has captured a piece or moved a pawn
  • If you can take one of the opposition players pieces you must
  • The king is a regular piece. No check or checkmate rules apply. It can take and be taken like a normal piece
  • If only 1 player can't make a legal move their turn is skipped
  • If you are able to take multiple of the opposition player's pieces you may take any piece you like
