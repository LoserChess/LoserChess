Rules:

  • The goal of the game is to be the first player to lose all of their pieces
  • All pieces move the same as in normal chess
  • No check or checkmate rules apply
  • The king acts like a regular piece, taking and being taken
  • If you are able to take one of the opposing pieces you must
  • If you are able to take multiple of the opposing pieces you may choose which piece you take
  • If 1 player can’t make a legal move their turn is skipped
  • If both players can’t make a legal move the game ends in a draw
  • If the same board position is repeated 3 times in 1 game the game ends in a draw
  • If no pawn has been moved or piece has been captured in the past 50 moves the game ends in a draw
